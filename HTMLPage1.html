About Us

Ecowrap is committed to reducing plastic waste with our biodegradable packaging solutions.

Plastic pollution is one of the biggest environmental challenges of our time. Traditional plastic takes hundreds of years to decompose, polluting our land and oceans. By switching to biodegradable packaging, we can significantly reduce our ecological footprint and protect the planet for future generations. At Ecowrap, we provide sustainable alternatives that break down naturally, ensuring a cleaner, greener world.
